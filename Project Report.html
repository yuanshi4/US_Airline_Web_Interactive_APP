1. Dataset Description¶

The data source (2015 Flight Delays and Cancellations) contains three datasets:

- Airlines.csv (14 rows and 2 columns)¶

IATA_CODE: Abbreviation of airline companies 
AIRLINE: Full name of airline companies

- Airports.csv (322 rows and 7 columns)¶

IATA_CODE: Abbreviation of airports (note this is different from IATA_CODE in Airlines.csv
AIRPORT: Full name of the airport
CITY: Name of the city which airport is located
STATE: State where the airport is located
COUNTRY: Country where the airport is located
LANTITUDE: lantitude of the airport
longitude: longitude of the airport

- Flights.csv(5,819,079 rows, 31 columns)¶

AIRLINE: Abbreviation of airline companies 
ORIGIN_AIRPORT: airport where the flight is flying from
DESTINATION_AIRPORT: airport where the flight is flying to
YEAR: Year of the record
MONTH: Month of the record
DAY: Date of the record
FLIGHT_NUMBER: Flight identifier 
etc....(other columns are not very revalent to our studies)

2. Question of Interest (Purpose of the web app)¶

The goal of our web app is:

For each airline that the user selects, plot a graph of the US map with points on the locations of airports that this airline flies to with an indicator on the number of flights coming in/out of that airport by the selected airline (size of the point or color of the point, etc.)


Expected User input: Airline company they are interested to check
Expected Output: A US map with plot that indicates the number of flights from the selected airline company across the airports in the US
